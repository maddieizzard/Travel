Suite Life of Mizz

About Me:

Hello, I am Maddie Izzard. In the Spring of 2023, I sailed with Semester at Sea on voyage 131. During my four months on the ship I learned so much about myself and the world around me. Through this blog I hope to share my love of travel and inspire others to explore new places!

Travel Tips:

  • Learn to accept when your itinerary has to change
  • Solo traveling, while scary, is amazing
  • Just book the experience
  • You will regret not doing something more than spending money on actually doing it
  • You can sleep when you get home
  • Learn the difference between travel and vacation and what you prefer
  • Do your research beforehand (transportation, big landmarks, currency)
  • When you think it is time to go home for the night, stay out a few minutes longer, that is when the best things happen
  • Journal to help you remember as much as you can
  • Take a lot of pictures
  • Take a lot of videos
  • â€¦ but be in the moment
  • Pack outfits, not individual clothes
  • Meet new people
  • Try new things
  • Bring a universal adapter
  • ESIMs are great (Airlo and Holafly are my favorite)
